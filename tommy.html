Web Design

TOMMY WAGNER

Tommy Wagner is an up-and-coming country music singer. Collaborating with a group, I designed a website for him for Info1300, “Designing and Programming for the Web”. This website was created using HTML5, CSS, JavaScript and PHP. It includes multiple interactive features such as a client-side and server-side form validation, image slideshows and various hover effects. By working with Tommy, we created a website that fit his style. Our target audience while designing the site was young adults age 18-24. We hoped to appeal to them in order to gain a larger audience for Tommy and showcase his talent.

Information Architecture

The site was split into four main sections: About, News, Music and Pictures. The about page features Tommy Wagner’s bio. The news page linked to articles highlighting Tommy’s success. The music page features videos of Tommy singing, as well as MP3s of his songs and a link to his Soundcloud. The Pictures page features pictures from Tommy’s social media.

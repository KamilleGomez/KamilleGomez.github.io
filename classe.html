Graphic Design

LABORATORY FOR ACCELERATOR-BASED SCIENCE & EDUCATION

I worked as a graphic design assistant for CLASSE. CLASSE stands for the Cornell Laboratory For Accelerator-Based Sciences and Education. CLASSE is home to the Cornell High Energy Synchrotron, one of the largest particle accelerators in the world. The particle accelerator attracts hundreds of talented scientist every year to CLASSE. As a graphic design assistant I worked on numerous projects, all aimed on generating awareness of the amazing scientific innovations that happen within CLASSE.

The Chess-U Upgrade

The Chess-U upgrade is a major project to renovate the CESR accelerator. I developed graphics in order to solicit additional funding from the state, emphasizing the fact that the upgrade will spur job growth. In a press conference, Lieutenant Governor Kathy Hochul of NY announced a $15 million grant for to be used to upgrade the Cornell High Energy Synchrotron Source. During this press conference, my designs were featured in the backdrop.
